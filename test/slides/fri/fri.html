What's FRI and Can it Help?

  • FRI allows us to stochastically compress a vector \( \mathbf{x} \)
  • We represent this compression as the function \( \Phi \)
$$ | \mathbf{x} | = N \quad \rightarrow \quad | \Phi(\mathbf{x}) | = m $$
$$ \mathbb{E}[\Phi(\mathbf{x})_i] = x_i $$
  • \( m \) is user defined
  • This means that we control the level of compression ⟶ we control the accuracy
  • What if we applied \( \Phi \) to \( t_{i j}^{a b} \)?
