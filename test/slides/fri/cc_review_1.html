Define a wave function

$$ \hat{T}_1 = \sum_{ai} t_i^a a^\dagger_a a_i \quad\quad \hat{T}_2 = \frac{1}{4} \sum_{aibj} t_{ij}^{ab} a^\dagger_a a_i a^\dagger_b a_j $$
$$ \hat{T}_1 = \sum_{ai} \bbox[5px,border:3px solid red]{ t_i^a } a^\dagger_a a_i \quad\quad \hat{T}_2 = \frac{1}{4} \sum_{aibj} \bbox[5px,border:3px solid red]{ t_{ij}^{ab} } a^\dagger_a a_i a^\dagger_b a_j $$

Define an energy

$$ E_{\text{HF}} + E_{\text{CCSD}} = \bra{\Psi} e^{-\hat{T}} \hat{H} e^{\hat{T}} \ket{\Psi} $$

Coupled Cluster Review

To get an energy, we need to solve for \( t_i^a \) and \( t_{ij}^{ab} \)

\( a,b,e,f,... \) virtual, \( i,j,m,n,... \) occupied

$$ \begin{aligned} t_{i}^{a} D_{i}^{a}=& f_{i a}+\sum_{e} t_{i}^{e} \mathscr{F}_{a e}-\sum_{m} t_{m}^{a} \mathscr{F}_{m i}+\sum_{m e} t_{i m}^{a e} \mathscr{F}_{m e} \\ &-\sum_{n f} t_{n}^{f}\langle n a \| i f\rangle-\frac{1}{2} \sum_{m e f} t_{i m}^{e f}\langle m a \| e f\rangle \\ &-\frac{1}{2} \sum_{m e n} t_{m n}^{a e}\langle n m \| e i\rangle \end{aligned} $$
$$ \begin{aligned} & t_{i j}^{a b} D_{i j}^{a b}= \langle i j \| a b\rangle+P_{-}(a b) \sum_{e} t_{i j}^{a e}\left(\mathscr{F}_{b e}-\frac{1}{2} \sum_{m} t_{m}^{b} \mathscr{F}_{m e}\right) \\ &-P_{-}(i j) \sum_{m} t_{i m}^{a b}\left(\mathscr{F}_{m j}+\frac{1}{2} \sum_{e} t_{j}^{e} \mathscr{F}_{m e}\right) \\ &+\frac{1}{2} \sum_{m n} \tau_{m n}^{a b} \mathscr{W}_{m n i j} + \frac{1}{2} \sum_{e f} \tau_{i j}^{e f} \mathscr{W}_{a b e f} \\ &+P_{-}(i j) P_{-}(a b) \sum_{m e}\left(t_{i m}^{a e} \mathscr{W}_{m b e j}-t_{i}^{e} t_{m}^{a}\langle m b \| e j\rangle\right) \\ &+P_{-}(i j) \sum_{e} t_{i}^{e}\langle a b \| e j\rangle-P_{-}(a b) \sum_{m} t_{m}^{a}\langle m b \| i j\rangle \end{aligned} $$
$$ \begin{aligned} & t_{i j}^{a b} D_{i j}^{a b}= \langle i j \| a b\rangle+P_{-}(a b) \sum_{e} t_{i j}^{a e}\left(\mathscr{F}_{b e}-\frac{1}{2} \sum_{m} t_{m}^{b} \mathscr{F}_{m e}\right) \\ &-P_{-}(i j) \sum_{m} t_{i m}^{a b}\left(\mathscr{F}_{m j}+\frac{1}{2} \sum_{e} t_{j}^{e} \mathscr{F}_{m e}\right) \\ &+\frac{1}{2} \sum_{m n} \tau_{m n}^{a b} \mathscr{W}_{m n i j} + \bbox[5px,border:3px solid red]{\frac{1}{2} \sum_{e f} \tau_{i j}^{e f} \mathscr{W}_{a b e f}} \\ &+P_{-}(i j) P_{-}(a b) \sum_{m e}\left(t_{i m}^{a e} \mathscr{W}_{m b e j}-t_{i}^{e} t_{m}^{a}\langle m b \| e j\rangle\right) \\ &+P_{-}(i j) \sum_{e} t_{i}^{e}\langle a b \| e j\rangle-P_{-}(a b) \sum_{m} t_{m}^{a}\langle m b \| i j\rangle \end{aligned} $$
